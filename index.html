Circulation of Indian Newspapers
across states and UTs: A Density projection

About the visualization
Newspapers are still at large in India despite the digital takeover. This visualization aims to portray the circulation of newspapers across different states and UTs(Union Territories) of India with a normalized population of 10,000 people each state. Data represents circulation in 2018.

Despite Uttar Pradesh having largest circulation of newspapers, with total circulation of 68 Million newspapers... density plots with normalized population shows Chandigarh having highest circulation, followed by Delhi and Uttarkhand. Many of the eastern states show the lowest circulations with Nagaland being lower end, followed by Assam and Meghalaya.

Data source:- OFFICE OF REGISTRAR OF NEWSPAPERS FOR INDIA

Density plots inspired by Federica Fragapane's Population Density.
